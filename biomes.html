Explore the Biomes of Noctarion

Biome One

A scrappy wasteland scattered with rusted iron, tangled wires, and broken tech. Navigate dim skies and dig through tungsten-rich debris for valuable parts.

Biome Two

A brighter landscape of cracked circuit boards and sandy terrain. Explore tan-green plains dotted with computer chip relics and tech ruins.

Biome Three

A shadowy, metallic valley pulsing with stored energy. Lithium-rich grounds and scattered batteries set the stage for your final challenge.
